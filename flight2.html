STARSHIP'S SECOND FLIGHT TEST

Starship Integrated Flight Test 2 is our second flight which will hopefully be successful. Check timeline for events.

Mission Overview

We are planning to have a soft splashdown in the Gulf Of Mexico for both the Super Heavy Booster and the Starship Upper Stage including an in-space relight of a raptor engine to demonstrate de-orbit burns. We are not doing any tricky test like disabling engines yet on these early flights. We wanna demonstrate how an operational launch would perform.

  • • Goal: Soft splashdown for both the Super Heavy Booster and the Starship Upper Stage.
  • • Payload: None.
  • • Target Orbit: Suborbital Trajectory.
  • • Recovery plan: We are not planning to recover any vehicle in this flight.
