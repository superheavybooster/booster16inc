STARSHIP'S FOURTH FLIGHT TEST

On Starship's Fourth Flight Test we are aiming on going on our planned suborbital trajectory this time around. For this flight we're also NOT aiming to do an in-space raptor relight to demonstrate de-orbit burns due to the last 2 flight failures. For booster we might be going for the catch this time, but if anything goes wrong we'll offshore into the beach. All that said let's hope for a successful flight this time around again.

Mission Overview

  • • Goal: Splashdown for both the Super Heavy Booster and the Starship Upper Stage in the Gulf Of Mexico.
  • • Payload: None
  • • Target Orbit: Suborbital Trajectory
  • • Recovery plan: We are not planning to recover any vehicle on this flight.
